I am also versatile in my programming approaches (object-oriented, functional, modular) to prioritise strategic, efficient and robust solutions.

With sharp communication and project management, I orchestrate multiple pipeline deliveries simultaneously. I'm particularly interested in reducing bias such that any solutions I create benefit as many people as possible.

I first entered the world of data in January 2023 via the Digital Futures training programme, from which I graduated with their highest ever intermediate Python exam result (98%). Those experiences laid the foundation for me to comfortably navigate stakeholder relationships such as resolving conflicts and escalating key dependencies, and to generate with new solutions from new perspectives in the face of setbacks.

My current role sits within the ESG segment of financial services, and I want to continue applying myself within organisations committed to improving their ethical and sustainable practices with the help of tech and data.
